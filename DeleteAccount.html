Option 1 — Delete it yourself in the app (instant)

You can permanently delete your account right inside Lumia:

  • Open Lumia and sign in
  • Go to Settings → About → Account
  • Tap Delete account → Delete forever

Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email us to delete my account

Or email pretlove63821@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account".

Send the request from the same email you used to sign in so we can verify it's you.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, profile photo)
  • Your subscription/plan status
  • Your cloud-backed chats
  • Your account itself

What may be retained

  • Chats stored only on your device are removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.
  • Limited records we are legally required to keep, for no longer than necessary.

How long it takes

In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are processed within 30 days.

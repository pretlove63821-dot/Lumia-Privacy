Privacy Policy

Lumia — AI Chat & Companions
Last updated: 4 June 2026 · Effective: 4 June 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumia ("Lumia", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Lumia mobile application (the "App"). Lumia lets you chat with AI characters and assistants. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

a) Account information

The App works without an account. If you choose to sign in with Google (through our authentication provider, Supabase), we receive and store:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (as provided by your Google profile)
  • Your profile photo (as provided by your Google profile)
  • A unique account identifier and your subscription plan status

We never receive or store your Google password.

b) Content you provide

  • Messages you type to AI characters
  • Images you upload for an AI character to view (available on paid plans)
  • Voice input, which is converted to text on your device using your device's speech-recognition feature; we receive the resulting text, not an audio recording
  • Custom characters you create (name, description, personality, and any image you add)

c) Usage & technical data

  • Message counts and limits used to manage free-tier allowances and ads
  • App preferences (theme, liked characters, message ordering)
  • Promo codes you redeem
  • Basic device and connection data necessary to deliver responses and, on the free tier, to serve ads

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI replies to your messages
  • To provide, maintain, and personalize the App
  • To sign you in and sync your chats across sessions (if you sign in)
  • To manage free-tier limits, ad refills, subscriptions, and promo codes
  • To keep the App secure and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your private chat content to train our own models.

4. AI Processing

When you send a message (and, on paid plans, an image), it is sent to our secure server and forwarded to a third-party AI provider to generate a response:

  • Groq — used to power responses on the free tier
  • Google (Gemini) — used to power responses, including image understanding, on paid tiers

These providers process your message solely to return a reply and handle it under their own privacy and security terms. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information (such as passwords, financial details, or government IDs) in your chats.

6. Advertising

The free tier of Lumia is supported by ads. Our advertising partners may collect device identifiers and similar data to deliver and measure ads in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can remove ads by upgrading to a paid plan when subscriptions become available.

7. Payments & Subscriptions

Paid plans (when launched) are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment card details — those are handled directly by Google Play.

8. Data Storage & Retention

  • On your device: Your chats, preferences, and custom characters are stored locally on your device. You can clear them at any time from within the App or by clearing the App's data.
  • Cloud backup: If you are signed in, your recent chats are backed up to our database and automatically deleted after approximately 3 days.
  • Account data: Retained while your account exists. When you request deletion, we remove your account data within a reasonable period.

9. Data Sharing

We share your information only:

  • With the service providers listed above, strictly to operate the App
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition), with notice where required

10. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (including under GDPR and CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Request a copy of your data (portability)
  • Withdraw consent or object to certain processing
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell your data)

You can sign out at any time, delete chats locally, and request account deletion using the contact details below.

Account & data deletion: To delete your account and associated data, email us at the address in the Contact section with the subject "Delete my account", from the email address linked to your Lumia account.

11. Children's Privacy

Lumia is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. Some characters and conversations are intended for mature audiences; if you are a minor, please do not use those features. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards — including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and trusted infrastructure providers — to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
